Job Tracker

Welcome to Job Tracker (Dispatch)

1. Set Up Your Profile

Click "Profile" in the top navigation to upload your master resume (PDF). We'll extract the text to help tailor future applications.

2. Track Jobs

Click "+ Add Job" to start tracking a new opportunity. You can drag and drop job cards between the columns below to update their status.

3. Generate AI Kits

Click any job card to view details. Use the "Generate Kit" button to let AI write a tailored cover letter and resume for that specific role.
